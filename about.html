THE STORY OF OUR SUCCESS

New Poona Bakery is a heritage brand that serves a wide range of sweet and savory delicacies. Right from fresh wholesome breads to rich velvety cakes, distinctively flaky cream rolls, buttery cookies and our star product – the Vegetable pattice; the list is endless!


What started as a single outlet in Pune city, has now spread to 100+ outlets in and around Pune.



50+ YEARS
100+ OUTLETS
200+ PRODUCT
ALWAYS FRESH

“Honestly, bakery or the hospitality industry never appealed to me. I was hundred percent sure I would never be working there. Today, I’m glad my father took the decision of pushing me into it. I wanted to be a doctor but fell short of 1 mark. It was my mother who pushed and encouraged me to take up the family business as a challenge.

In the late 80’s, Pune’s business owners took pride in not having any branches. We were one of the first ones to make a change. We started with 3 owned branches and today we have 100 outlets including franchisees.”

- Mr. Bhushan R Giramkar
Owner, New Poona Bakery
